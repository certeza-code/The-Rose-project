The Rose Tony Foundation is a non governmental organisation which is focused on awarding scholarships to indigenous students who have proven and distinguished themselves to be academically inclined. The foundation was founded by Lady Rose Chukwu together with the husband High Chief Tony Chukwu a.k.a onweghi ihe kariri Chineke in 2007 and over the years , it has recorded tremendous increase in the number of students awarded the scholarship annually. The foundation is a reminder that there is always the need to give back to the society as there is more reward and sense of fulfillment in giving. THe foundation has helped in so many ways to alleviate much burdens from parents who cannot afford to train their children in the tertiary institution. It has also helped to reduce the rate of illiteracy among indegenous students. Apply For Scholarship

The Rose Tony Foundation awards an annual scholarship to indigenous students who successfully writes and passes the foundation's exam. The scholarship is basically for undergraduates who are indigenes of Ehime Mbano LGA in Imo State and Afikpo South in Ebonyi State of Nigeria respectively. The aforementioned indigenes must be an undergraduate student of a known tertiary institution in Nigeria. Our scholarship scheme spans within Nigeria only.The beneficiaries are awarded the sum of one hundred thousand naira(#100,000)per academic session from the time of gaining the scholarship till he or she graduates from the tertiary institution.

Requirements For Application

The following are the requirements needed from students who wish to apply for the scholarship;

  • A photocopy of your school admission letter
  • A photocopy of your JAMB UTME result slip
  • A photocopy of JAMB admission letter
  • A photocopy of your WAEC result
  • A photocopy of your acceptance fee receipt
  • Certificate of Local Government of Origin
  • Two passport sized photograph
  • An application letter explaining why you need the scholarship.

The requirements are to be submitted to Rev Fr Stan, the parish priest of St. Michael's Catholic Church, umueze1 via +2348034970774 or click here to submit online.
