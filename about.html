OUR PRESIDENT

Lolo Rose Tony Chukwu is a selfless, humble , understnding, loving lady who took it upon herself to ensure that youths gain access to formal eductaion. Lolo Rose Tony Chukwu first launched the schorlarship foundation in 2007 at Umuihechukwu Umueze1 with twelve scholars as a pilot take off, then the following year, eight more schorlars were awarded where the the scholars were Umueze1 indigenes only. The inherent financial burden cou;ld not deter her ambition to extend the scheme to her biological ghome(Afikpo) and the enlarged Ehime Mbano society hence a fresh approach that involved the aptly organised aptitude test to ensure that the best crop of students earn the university sponsorship which motivated her dynamic husband Chief Tony Chukwu to join in sharing in the adventure. Currently, the foundation can boast of over two hundred students who are undoubtedly excelling in their various fields.
