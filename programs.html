THE AWARDING OF SCHORLARSHIP

The Rose Tony Foundation hosted her 13th schorlarship award on the 31st of December,2019 which recorded the presence of very distinguished dignitaries who showed their massive support for the Foundation.There were over 60 scholars that were awarded the schorlarship. These schorlars were able to proof their intelligence both in the written and oral exams.
